Indian Chicken Curry

Home

Ingredients (1x)

  • 2 pounds skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup cooking oil
  • 1 ½ cups chopped onion
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 ½ teaspoons minced fresh ginger root
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 (15 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup water
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Season chicken pieces with 1 teaspoon salt.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Cook chicken until lightly browned, about 10 minutes. Remove chicken.
  3. In the same skillet, cook onion, garlic, and ginger until onion is tender, about 5 minutes. Add curry powder, cumin, turmeric, coriander, and cayenne and cook for 2 minutes. Stir in 1 tablespoon of water and cook for another minute.
  4. Return chicken to skillet and add tomatoes, yogurt, 1 tablespoon cilantro, and 1 teaspoon salt. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and cover. Simmer for 20-25 minutes.
  5. Stir in 1/2 cup water and garam masala. Cover and simmer for another 10-15 minutes.
  6. Stir in 1 tablespoon cilantro and lemon juice.
